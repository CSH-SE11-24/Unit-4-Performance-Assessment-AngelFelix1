MV Agusta Turismo Veloce RC

The MV Agusta Turismo Veloce RC model comes with a unique design package that features cast wheels, a red frame, and high-contrast graphics. The bike is the result of years of development.

BMW K1600GTL

For those who want to tour the open road in luxurious comfort, the BMW K1600GTL is a top performer. A sense of ease, comfort, and smoothness shine through when riding the K1600GTL. The 1649cc inline-six engine is incredibly quiet, creating more of a pleasurable cruise experience when logging the miles. Twist the throttle and enjoy the smooth acceleration.

Indian Roadmaster Limited

Take your motorcycle touring to the next level on the Indian Roadmaster Limited. Slip into the seat and feel your entire body relax in the plush seats with individual heat controls. The low handlebars, wide seat, and generous floorboards give you and your passenger plenty of room. Use the adjustable electronic windshield to position it at the perfect height.

Moto Guzzi MGX-21

When the prototype of the Moto Guzzi MGX-21 first came out, industry people loved it but thought it would never actually get produced. Boy, were they wrong. Moto Guzzi managed to craft a luxury bagger that is worthy of competing in the American market.

Triumph Rocket 3 GT

The Rocket 3 comes in two models, the R and the GT. The Triumph Rocket 3 GT has the handlebars moved back more for a more upright riding position. This creates a more comfortable body position that is less sporty. The GT also has luxury features you won’t see on the Rocket 3 R model.

BMW K1600 Grand America

The BMW K1600 Grand America ranks at the top of the touring category next to the Honda Gold Wing. While BMW never makes any major changes, it does continually improve year after year. You’ll enjoy the latest technology, such as adaptive headlights that swivel to match the bike’s turning and suspension to continuously illuminate the road in front of you.
